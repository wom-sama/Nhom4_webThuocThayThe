# Product Vision

## Vision

Xay dung mot website ho tro nguoi dung va duoc si tra cuu thuoc, kiem tra ton kho va nhan de xuat thuoc thay the co giai thich khi thuoc chinh khong co san.

He thong uu tien:

- De xuat minh bach dua tren hoat chat, ham luong, dang bao che, ton kho va gia.
- Canh bao thuoc ke don, di ung hoat chat va chong chi dinh.
- Quyen truy cap phu hop cho user, duoc si, chuyen gia va admin.
- Co dau vet review chuyen gia, audit log, test result va backup metadata.

## Product Scope

Chuc nang san pham hien tai:

1. Dang nhap va phan quyen theo vai tro.
2. Tra cuu thuoc theo ten, hoat chat va danh muc.
3. Quan ly danh muc thuoc va kho/lo thuoc.
4. De xuat thuoc thay the co score, ly do va canh bao.
5. Danh gia ket qua de xuat boi duoc si/chuyen gia.
6. Dashboard ton kho, audit log, nguon du lieu ngoai va backup metadata.
7. Kiem thu acceptance, security va performance tu dong.

Ngoai pham vi cua increment hien tai:

- Tu dong chan doan benh.
- Tu dong thay the chi dinh cua bac si.
- Thanh toan hoac ban thuoc truc tuyen.
- Bao cao do an, slide va video demo.

## Success Criteria

- Luong Panadol het hang de xuat duoc Paracetamol DHG va cac ung vien phu hop.
- Score va ly do de xuat hien thi ro.
- Ho so di ung tao canh bao muc cao.
- Chuc nang quan tri bi gioi han theo role.
- Acceptance, security va performance suite dat 100%.
- Moi thay doi co Jira issue key trong branch/commit/PR.
