# Use Case Summary

## UC-01 Dang Nhap Va Phan Quyen

Actor: Nguoi dung, Admin.

Luong chinh:

1. Nguoi dung mo trang dang nhap.
2. Nhap email va mat khau.
3. He thong xac thuc tai khoan mau.
4. He thong tao cookie auth voi role.
5. Nguoi dung duoc chuyen ve trang tra cuu.

Ngoai le:

- Sai email/mat khau: hien thi loi.
- Tai khoan khoa: khong cho dang nhap.
- Khong du quyen: tra ve Access Denied.

## UC-02 Quan Ly Danh Muc Thuoc

Actor: Admin, Duoc si.

Luong chinh:

1. Admin/Duoc si mo man hinh danh muc thuoc.
2. Xem danh sach thuoc va thong tin co ban.
3. Them/sua thuoc voi danh muc, dang bao che, don vi, nha san xuat.
4. He thong validation gia, ten, ham luong va trang thai.
5. Danh sach duoc cap nhat trong kho du lieu tam.

## UC-03 Quan Ly Kho Va Lo Thuoc

Actor: Admin, Duoc si.

Luong chinh:

1. Mo man hinh kho.
2. Xem ton kho tong hop theo thuoc.
3. Them lo thuoc voi so lo, kho, so luong, han su dung.
4. He thong khong chap nhan so luong am.
5. Ton kho tong hop cap nhat theo lo.

## UC-04 Tra Cuu Thuoc

Actor: Khach, Nguoi dung, Duoc si.

Luong chinh:

1. Nguoi dung nhap tu khoa.
2. He thong tim theo ten, hoat chat, danh muc, nha san xuat.
3. Hien thi danh sach thuoc kem ton kho.
4. Nguoi dung mo chi tiet thuoc.

Ngoai le:

- Khong co ket qua: hien thi thong bao va goi y tim bang hoat chat.
- Thuoc het hang: hien thi trang thai de sprint 2 goi y thay the.
